## Q-01 — What counts as an "impossible scenario" for BR-08? 🔴

**Source:** `SKILL.md:19`, `domain.md:D-04`

**Gap:** BR-08 says "no error handling for impossible scenarios." But "impossible" is not defined. The LLM must infer whether a scenario can occur based on its context window, which may not include the full call graph.

**Question for maintainer:** Should BR-08 be scoped to "impossible given the function's stated contract" (i.e., preconditions the caller guarantees)? Or does it mean "impossible given the entire system state"? The former is safely determinable from local code; the latter requires global knowledge the LLM rarely has.
